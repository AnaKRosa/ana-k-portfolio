The Challenge I needed a portfolio that looked like mine — and that I could build.

As a web and graphic designer, my portfolio is my most important piece of work. But I didn't want to be limited by no-code tools or dependent on a developer to make changes. I wanted full ownership — design and code.

The goal wasn't just a good-looking site. It was proof that I can take a project from concept to production without handing it off to anyone.

Design Process

I started in Figma by defining the look and feel — dark hero, lavender cards, editorial typography, and a tone that feels personal but professional. Then I iterated across multiple versions before touching a single line of code.

Vibe Coding

Design and build — no developer needed

Vibe coding means using AI as a development partner — you direct, it executes. I described each section, made decisions on spacing, components, and behavior, and iterated in real time. Every pixel reflects a deliberate creative choice.

This is the skill that sets me apart: I can take a design from Figma to a live, functional website myself — which means faster delivery, lower cost, and zero translation errors for my clients.
